💰 Student Crew Coins

What are Coins?

Coins are a special currency within Student Crew that lets you boost visibility, promote items, or unlock premium features.

How to Earn Coins

  • 🎉 Invite friends to join the platform
  • 📤 Successfully post an item for sale
  • ✅ Complete a transaction with good feedback

How to Use Coins

  • 🚀 Boost a listing to appear on top for 48 hours
  • 🌟 Highlight an item with a glowing effect
  • 🔓 Unlock additional chat requests or categories

Important

Coins are tied to your profile and are not transferable or refundable. Abuse may lead to suspension.
